Detailed Description

A class that controls the procedural generation of a Maze, the enemies and collectables inside of it.

It is able to generate a solvable (non-looping, no cut off sections) maze of any size, filled with gems (collectables) and enemies. The amount of enemies, collectables and distance between them is customisable.
